Benefits & Discounts

The Kalinga Senior Portal provides a variety of benefits and discounts designed to support senior citizens in their daily lives. These include financial assistance, healthcare support, utility discounts, and lifestyle privileges to help improve quality of life.

Social Pension (DSWD)

Monthly financial assistance for qualified senior citizens under DSWD program.

Utility Discount

Discount on electricity and water bills to help reduce monthly expenses.

OSCA Programs

Medical assistance, vaccines, and health services provided by OSCA.

Burial Assistance

Financial aid for funeral and burial expenses of senior citizens.

Medicine Discount Program

Discount on prescribed medicines with OSCA booklet and valid prescription.

Grocery Discount

Discount on essential groceries with senior booklet registration and monitoring.

Medical Discount

Discount on hospital bills, check-ups, and laboratory services.

Restaurant Discount

Special discounts on food establishments and dining services.

Transportation Discount

Reduced fares on public transport for easier and affordable travel.

Leisure Discount

Discounts on cinemas, hotels, resorts, and recreational activities.

How to use the Portal

1

Login to your Account

To access the system, go to the Login page and enter the email address you used to sign up. Carefully type your password into the password box. Once your details are entered, click the Login button to enter the system and view your documents.

2

Sign up guide

To create a new account, start by clicking the Sign Up button on the screen. First, type your full name into the name box. Next, enter your email address and create a password that you can easily remember. Type that same password again in the confirmation box to make sure it is correct. Finally, click the button at the bottom to finish creating your account.

3

Explore Services

Click on the 'Services' menu to find medical assistance, pension updates, and community events.

4

Update Profile

Keep your emergency contact information and medical records updated in 'Settings' for your safety.

5

How to Search

Go to Secure and Storage, scroll down, and you will find the Search Bar. Then, type the document name to find your documents.
